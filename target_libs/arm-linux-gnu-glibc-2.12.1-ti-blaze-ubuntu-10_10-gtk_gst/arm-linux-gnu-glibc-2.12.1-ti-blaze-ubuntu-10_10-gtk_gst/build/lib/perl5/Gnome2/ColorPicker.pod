=head1 PROPERTIES

=over

=item 'alpha' (Glib::UInt : readable / writable)

The alpha value of the chosen color

=item 'blue' (Glib::UInt : readable / writable)

The amount of blue in the chosen color

=item 'dither' (boolean : readable / writable)

Whether or not to dither to color

=item 'green' (Glib::UInt : readable / writable)

The amount of green in the chosen color

=item 'red' (Glib::UInt : readable / writable)

The amount of red in the chosen color

=item 'title' (string : readable / writable)

The title to give to the color picker

=item 'use-alpha' (boolean : readable / writable)

Whether or not to give the color an alpha value

=back
